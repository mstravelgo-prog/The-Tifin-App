How it works

Simple as ordering
ghar ka khana

Two kinds of people make TiffinBox work — those who order, and those who cook.

For customers
1
Browse nearby partners
See homemakers in your area with menus, prices, and ratings. Filter by meal type — breakfast, lunch, or dinner.
2
Pick a plan
Order for 1 day to try, or subscribe for 3, 7, or 30 days and save up to 20%.
3
Pay once, eat daily
Pay via UPI, card, or cash on delivery. Get fresh tiffin at your door every day at the time you chose.
4
Rate your meal
After each delivery, rate your experience. Your feedback helps homemakers grow and keeps quality high.
For homemakers
1
Register as a partner
Sign up with your phone number. Add your photo, speciality dishes, and the area you can deliver to.
2
Add your menu & prices
List your tiffin items with prices and photos. Toggle items on/off daily based on what you're making.
3
Cook & deliver
Accept orders, cook as usual, and deliver yourself or hand off to a delivery partner. You control everything.
4
Get paid weekly
85% of every order goes directly to you. Payments are settled every week to your UPI or bank account.
